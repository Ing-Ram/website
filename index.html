Chad Ingram

Music | Technology | Travel | Food

Chad is a Music Director, previously a Software Engineer, and dedicated father. With a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, he has built a career that bridges both the technical and creative worlds. Outside of work, Chad finds joy in playing the trumpet in a few local orchestras in the Bay Area, embracing the artistry of live performance. An avid traveler, he loves exploring new destinations with his family whenever and wherever possible, creating lifelong memories along the way.
