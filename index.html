About Chad

Chad is a multifaceted individual whose life is a blend of technology, creativity, and family. As a devoted father and husband, he cherishes the moments spent with his loved ones, always striving to create meaningful experiences and lasting memories.

With a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science, Chad has built a career as a Software Engineer. However, his ambitions extend far beyond coding and development. He continuously strives to bridge the worlds of technology and the creative arts, seeking opportunities to innovate and integrate both disciplines in meaningful ways. Whether through developing software solutions that enhance artistic expression or finding new ways to use technology in music and performance, his work reflects a deep passion for both logic and creativity.

Outside of his professional endeavors, Chad finds joy in playing the trumpet in several local orchestras in the Bay Area. Through live performance, he embraces the artistry of music, immersing himself in the power and emotion that only live orchestral music can provide. His love for music isn’t just a hobby—it’s an essential part of his identity, allowing him to connect with audiences and fellow musicians on a profound level.

Beyond work and music, Chad is an avid traveler with an insatiable curiosity for exploring new places. Whether it’s a weekend getaway or an international adventure, he relishes the chance to experience different cultures, cuisines, and landscapes. Traveling with his family makes these experiences even more meaningful, as he believes that discovering the world together strengthens their bond and creates lifelong memories.

Through it all, Chad remains committed to personal and professional growth, constantly seeking ways to evolve, innovate, and inspire—whether through technology, music, or the adventures that life brings.
